# Voicescape

A web-based virtual launchpad art installation for composing "music" with recordings of young people thinking out loud, to "illustrate" the inner voice inside your head.

You can compose "your art" by triggering pre-recorded word/phrase clips over a background track using their keyboard or a mobile tap grid. You can record your own voice to add custom clips, and assign them to keys. You may also download a MP3 of your work locally.

Your voice recording and the MP3 are all stored locally. Nothing is uploaded to the internet.

We do however ask for some non-identifying information to help us better understand the demographics of our visitors.

The background music clip was created by [Charlie Brown](https://linktr.ee/charlieb_composerandproducer). Some voice clips were recorded by [Alice Evans](http://www.aliceevansfineart.co.uk) from participants' transcripts.
